Who Is Nezuko?

Mmmph! Mmm mmph! (I am Nezuko Kamado, Tanjiro's younger sister who was turned into a demon but retained my humanity!)

Even though I'm a demon, I've never eaten a human. Instead, I fight alongside my brother to protect innocent people from other demons who have lost their way.

I sleep to regain my strength instead of eating humans, and I've grown stronger through my love for my family and determination to help others.

My Demon Powers

As a demon, I have unique abilities that I use to protect humans and fight evil demons.

My special powers include:

  • Blood Demon Art: Exploding Blood - I can ignite my blood to create powerful explosions
  • Size Manipulation - I can shrink to fit in small spaces or grow larger for battle
  • Regeneration - I can heal from injuries quickly like other demons
  • Enhanced Strength - My demon strength allows me to kick through walls and fight powerful enemies
  • Sunlight Resistance - Unlike other demons, I can walk in sunlight without being harmed

My bamboo muzzle helps me control my demon instincts and reminds me to protect humans instead of harming them.

My Precious Memories

Life Before

I remember my happy life with my family, taking care of my younger siblings and helping around the house. Those warm memories keep my humanity alive.

Tanjiro's Love

My brother never gave up on me, even when everyone said I should be killed. His love and faith in me gives me strength to fight my demon nature.

Protecting Others

Every time I protect a human from demons, I feel more like myself. Fighting for others instead of my own hunger makes me stronger.
